Financial Services

A Large Retail Bank — governed agentic analytics across three regulated divisions.

The Challenge

Compliance, retail banking, and risk all needed access to overlapping datasets — but with strictly different permission scopes. Open AI tools were out of the question: data must never leave the bank's network perimeter, and regulators require full audit trails on every answer.

How Quaeris Helped

Quaeris deployed on-premise (Kubernetes, within the bank's VPC). The Bring-Your-Own-Model (BYOM) capability let the bank connect an approved internal LLM rather than a third-party cloud model. Role-based access was configured per division; row-level and field-level masking enforced at query time. Every query, answer, user, and data access is logged in a tamper-evident audit trail.

The Result

Audit preparation time was substantially reduced — regulators could access lineage on demand rather than waiting two weeks for the data team to reconstruct a query trail. Compliance and risk now self-serve within governed access boundaries; the bank's IT team retains full control of the model and data perimeter.

FAQ

Questions we get
from data leaders.

Can Quaeris be deployed in weeks, not months?

Yes. Because Quaeris is warehouse-native, there is no ETL to rebuild and no data to move. The deployment model is: connect to your existing Snowflake, BigQuery, Databricks, or Redshift; certify core metrics in the semantic layer; configure role-based access. Most customers reach their first governed answer within three weeks.

What happens to my existing BI tools — Tableau, Looker, Power BI?

Quaeris sits above your warehouse as a governed natural-language query layer. Your existing BI dashboards continue to work; Quaeris handles the ad-hoc question volume that currently clogs your data team's backlog. See how it compares: vs Tableau · vs Looker · vs Power BI.

How does Quaeris handle regulated data in financial services, insurance, and healthcare?

Three mechanisms: (1) Warehouse-native deployment — your data never moves to a third-party cloud; (2) Role-based access control enforced at query time, including row- and field-level masking; (3) A tamper-evident audit trail on every query, user, answer, and data access. Compliance documentation is available on request — contact us for current certification details.

Can I use my own LLM rather than a cloud AI provider?

Yes. Quaeris's Bring-Your-Own-Model (BYOM) capability lets you connect any compatible LLM — including internally hosted models — so your data and queries never leave your network perimeter. This is a common requirement in banking and insurance deployments.

How do I get the anonymized case studies de-anonymized for due diligence?

Several of our existing customers are willing to speak directly under NDA. Book a demo and our solutions engineering team can arrange a peer reference call with a company in your industry and warehouse environment.

What warehouses does Quaeris support?

Snowflake, BigQuery, Databricks, Azure Synapse, Amazon Redshift, and other major warehouse platforms. Quaeris is warehouse-native — it queries directly from your existing infrastructure, which means no data duplication, no latency from ETL, and a schema we work with, not around.
