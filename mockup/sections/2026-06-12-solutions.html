Why teams choose Quaeris

The three breakdowns that governed agentic analytics fixes.

Conflicting numbers across teams

Every team has its own definition of revenue, activation rate, or churn. Quaeris's Smart Semantic Layer certifies one definition and enforces it across every agent answer — so the board debate stops.

Analysts buried in ad-hoc requests

Business users wait days for answers that take minutes to retrieve. Quaeris gives any user a governed natural-language interface to their own data — agents answer; analysts focus on work that compounds.

AI that can't be audited or trusted

Most AI analytics tools are model-locked and produce outputs with no traceable lineage. Quaeris maintains a prompt-level audit trail and supports BYOM — so your compliance team can verify every answer and swap models as standards evolve.

Model independence

Quaeris connects to OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and Meta models. You choose which model processes your data — and you switch as the landscape evolves. No vendor lock-in means your AI governance policy isn't hostage to a single provider's roadmap. This is BYOM framed as a compliance feature, not a procurement one.

A semantic layer that learns your business — without a modeling sprint.

Every other semantic layer requires your team to write LookML, dbt metrics, or MDX cube definitions before the first query runs.
